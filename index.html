Nestle Product Analysis

This project looked into product performance in Nestle food company. The company is looking to expand its business by adding new product lines. Hence the need to know the performance of the current products. We discovered through analysis and visualization their top selling product, least selling product and peak sales periods. Through recommendations given, product performance was seen to improve by an average of 20% in various locations. This analysis was performed using PowerBI

Furniture Sales Analysis Using SQL

This project addresses the sales challenges faced by an eco-friendly furniture company which has struggled with understanding customer preferences and managing inventory due to inadequate sales data insights. To address these challenges and improve strategic decision-making and marketing, SQL queries were implemented to analyze sales trends, customer preferences and product performance. This enabled the company to make informed decisions on inventory management, marketing strategies, and customer engagement leading to a 10% increase in sales

Radox Airlines

Radox airlines a growing startup encountered significant challenges accessing internal data due to the fragmented nature of its business structure and systems. This lack of connectivity necessitated a comprehensive analysis of the available data to derive actionable insights aimed at propelling business growth and enhancing market positioning within the industry. Thus this project used Excel to analyze the available data and extract key insights. These insights included operational cost and revenue generated by flight routes, most travelled flight routes among other key metrics. Insights lead to route optimization and an increase in customer satisfaction and patronage by 15%.
